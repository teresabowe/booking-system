Our Location. Smithfield, a vibrant and well-connected place.

Our office space is located at Smithfield Square, Dublin, D07 SSXX. Back in the 17th century, Smithfield was a marketplace for trading livestock and other goods. Today, it offers a central location, a stop on the Red Luas Line and a wide range of independent establishments like the Light House Cinema, Token, Generator Hostel and the Jameson Distillery.

Transport Links. Close to all primary transport services.

The Luas light rail system that connects Dublins suburbs to its core. There are two lines: the Green Line runs from Broombridge in the north to Brides Glen in the south and the Red Line runs from Saggart in the west to The Point in the east. Dublin bus connects most parts of the city through a network of over 100 routes.

Eating out. Cafes and Restaurants in abundance.

To start off your day around Smithfield, grab a coffee in Proper Order Coffee Co on Haymarket St. Open since 2016. Once you've given your pastry and coffee an hour to digest, walk around the corner to Urbanity for brunch. Head to Fish Shop for dinner. Depending on your mood, you can either go to the informal Benburb Street location for fish, chips and natural wine, or book a table at the Queen Street location for the set menu, showcasing the best Irish seafood they've been able to get their hands on that week. To end this most amazing of days, head to dark Dublin dive Frank Ryans for a pint and a game of pool, or to The Cobblestone which has trad music sessions every night.
